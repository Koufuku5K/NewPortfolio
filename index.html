Featured project

Procedural Content Generation

This dissertation explores and investigates on using Convex Hull algorithm to introduce loop structures to procedurally generated dungeons, with the aim of enhancing the gameplay experience presented by dungeon exploration games. As a result of applying the Convex Hull algorithm, a dungeon with cyclic loops between rooms can be produced while still maintaining great efficiency.

This study provides other researchers with information that could lead to further development of the Procedural Content Generation (PCG) field in video games. Consequently, leading to the creation of more engaging games and ultimately improving the industry.
